Infrastructure

Our school provides world-class infrastructure spread across a 9-acre campus in Mannachanallur. We've created an environment that nurtures learning with modern facilities designed to inspire academic excellence and overall development.

Expansive Campus

School situated on a 9-acre campus in Mannachanallur with highly ventilated and spacious classrooms.

Modern Computer Labs

Fully air-conditioned computer laboratory with internet facility and latest systems for digital learning.

Audio-Visual Lab

Hi-Fi audio-visual lab for linguistic development and a magnificent mental maths lab.

Extensive Library

Well-established central library with encyclopedias, periodicals and scientific journals.

Expert Faculty

Qualified, efficient teachers with dedication, maintaining a limited number of students per class.

Kindergarten Facilities

Hygienically maintained, extensively equipped kindergarten classrooms for our youngest learners.

Pure Drinking Water

Purified drinking water available throughout the campus using advanced RO system.

Science Laboratories

Well-equipped science laboratories for practical learning and experimentation.

Language Resources

PC 5th dictionary software installed on each floor to enrich vocabulary.

Safe Environment

Fully CCTV-surveilled campus ensuring safety and security for all students.

SVR Matriculation Higher Secondary School is committed to providing quality education that nurtures academic excellence, personal growth, and social responsibility.
